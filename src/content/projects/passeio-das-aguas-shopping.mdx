Center-west Brazil's largest shopping center — a $232M, 1.94M sq ft program
delivered from green-field ground-up through 30 October 2013 opening, then
operated through a multi-year tenant-improvement phase.

The design-management scope coordinated dozens of specialty consultants and
trade contractors — façade, MEP, vertical conveyors, fire protection,
landscape, lighting, acoustics, signage — under a single governance rhythm.
A 4&nbsp;%/36&nbsp;%/60&nbsp;% change-cost split (absorbed / diverted /
transferred) and a tenant-committee workflow with explicit gates kept
project cost discipline through inauguration.

The tenant-improvement operational framework Sarah developed during this
program was adopted across Aliansce Sonae's national portfolio.

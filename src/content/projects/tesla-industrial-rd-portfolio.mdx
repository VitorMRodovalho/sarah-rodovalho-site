Industrial delivery for advanced manufacturing rewards owners who can move
between strategic capital decisions and the operational realities of an
active factory floor. Tesla's industrial portfolio spans short-cycle
workplace fit-outs, long-cycle R&D infrastructure, and the cell-development
work that supports manufacturing programs.

The Megapack engagement reduced design fees ~20% through scope alignment,
vendor consolidation, and a tighter definition of what "complete" meant for
each design phase — a useful pattern in industrial delivery where design fee
inflation is often a symptom of scope ambiguity rather than scope growth.

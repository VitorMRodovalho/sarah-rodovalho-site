Multi-region, multi-vendor hyperscale construction means change is constant —
the question is whether change becomes signal or noise. A consistent
change-order intake, classification, cost-impact, and approval cadence makes
the difference, particularly when 8 buildings are under construction in
parallel across three jurisdictions.

The change-order management log Sarah implemented was adopted program-wide
within CO+I — moving the team from project-local to program-consistent
governance.

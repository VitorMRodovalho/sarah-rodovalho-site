Healthcare architecture is unforgiving: a single bad adjacency in a clinical
plan compounds across every staff handoff and patient flow for the life of
the building. Array Architects' practice focuses exclusively on this domain,
which lets a designer specialize at the level of detail where the work is
most consequential.

The bed-tower and renovation scope Sarah contributed to combined new
construction with active-facility renovation — work that requires phased
schedules tuned to clinical operations and infection-control during
construction.

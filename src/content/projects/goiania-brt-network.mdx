The visible work was design management across the full corridor —
terminals, stations, and ground-up infrastructure. The defining moment was
a two-month critical-criteria window: targeted design revisions that
bypassed eminent-domain constraints without triggering land disputes, and
R$1.5M in design-change savings absorbed before they reached construction.

campus capacity translates into delivery decisions on long-lead equipment,
power-train coordination, and design-to-construction governance.

operations teams; alignment with general contractors and trade partners;
scope tracking across concurrent buildings; OFE strategy and long-lead
procurement support; and the resolution of design-construction friction
before it reaches the field.
